# Chore Tracker — Feature Ideas Backlog

Features brainstormed on 2026-04-15 but not yet scheduled for implementation.

---

## Kid Engagement

### 1. Chore streaks
Show a "streak" counter when a kid completes chores on consecutive days or weeks. Visual reward for consistency and habit-building.

### 2. Savings goals
Kids set a savings goal (e.g. "New LEGO set - $25") and see a progress bar as their balance grows toward it. Makes earnings feel concrete and purposeful.

### 3. Photo proof of completion
Kids can snap or upload a photo when marking a chore done. Photo appears during admin review as evidence and makes the completion feel more substantial.

---

## Admin UX

### 7. Batch approval
Multi-select or "Approve all" on the completion review page. Reduces click fatigue for parents reviewing many completions at once.

### 8. Admin notifications (email/push)
Notify admin when completions are waiting for review, rather than requiring them to manually check the app.

### 9. Admin dashboard summary stats
Quick-glance panel: pending approval count, total owed to all kids, completions submitted this week. Currently requires navigating to separate pages.

---

## Visibility & Reporting

### 10. Earnings history chart
Per-kid chart showing weekly or monthly earnings over time so parents can see effort trends.

### 11. Completion rate stats
What percentage of assigned chores does each kid actually complete? Weekly breakdown per user.

### 12. CSV/PDF export
Export chore and payment history for record-keeping or allowance reconciliation.

---

## Flexibility / Chore Model

### 13. Chore difficulty tiers
Label chores Easy / Medium / Hard. Could be informational or drive a multiplier bonus.

### 14. One-time scheduled chores
Chores that only appear on a specific calendar date (e.g. "Thanksgiving dishes - $3, Nov 28").

### 15. Chore notes / instructions
Rich text instructions visible to kids before they start, so they know exactly what "clean bathroom" means.

---

## Platform / Quality of Life

### 16. Dark/light mode toggle
Currently appears to be dark-only. A light mode option for daytime use.

### 17. PWA / "Add to home screen"
Make the app installable as a home screen icon so kids tap it directly rather than navigating to a URL.

### 18. Multi-admin support
Allow a second parent to log in with their own OIDC account and also approve / manage chores.
